Bendy & Muddy Went To Work.
September 19, 2013
We were on our way to work, and we were ready.
We were going to work very hard, but first Muddy wanted some breakfast.
Then we would have gone to work but Muddy wanted to play pool.
And then he found that Yak.
And then he wanted something from the claw machine.
I had to teach that
claw a lesson.
Then we were finally ready to work when Muddy found that robot game.
Then
we made one
more stop to
meet Freddie.
Then it was time to go home.
Work wasn't so hard.
We wanted to do it again the next day.
